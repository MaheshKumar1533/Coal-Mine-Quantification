ALIGNING WITH SDG 12

Goal 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

Our commitment to reducing carbon emissions from coal mining activities is directly aligned with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goal 12 (SDG 12). This goal focuses on ensuring sustainable consumption and production patterns by reducing waste, managing resources efficiently, and adopting environmentally-friendly practices.

Key Initiatives Aligned with SDG 12

Reducing Carbon Footprint: Implementing advanced technologies to minimize emissions in extraction, transportation, and processing activities.

Monitoring and Transparency: Providing real-time data on air quality and carbon emissions to promote transparency and informed decision-making.

Promoting Carbon Neutrality: Offering solutions for carbon offset through afforestation and adoption of renewable energy sources, such as solar and wind power.

Education and Awareness: Informing stakeholders about the importance of sustainable practices and the steps they can take to contribute to carbon neutrality.

By integrating these sustainable practices, we not only contribute to global environmental goals but also ensure a healthier and more resilient ecosystem for future generations.
